About Us

Welcome to MathGen! We are Shay Manor and Elan Smyla, two passionate individuals hailing from the Bay Area. Growing up in the heart of Silicon Valley, we were surrounded by cutting-edge technology and innovation, which profoundly shaped our perspectives and aspirations. Our journey began at Purdue University, where our shared enthusiasm for mathematics and technology brought us together.

Prior to college, we both took AP Computer Science in high school, which sparked our passion for coding and problem-solving. During our time at Purdue, we immersed ourselves in our studies, gaining invaluable knowledge and insights into the world of mathematics and programming.

Motivated by a desire to make math more accessible and engaging, we dedicated ourselves to extensive research and development. This program is the culmination of our efforts, designed to help users input complex math equations and receive detailed video explanations. We believe that understanding math should be an enjoyable experience, and we are excited to share our creation with you. Join us on this journey as we continue to explore the fascinating world of mathematics!

Feel free to explore the functionalities we offer and reach out to us for any inquiries!
